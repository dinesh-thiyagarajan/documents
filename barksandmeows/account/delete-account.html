Delete Your Barks and Meows Account

⚠️ Important Notice: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this page carefully before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account and all associated data from the Barks and Meows app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Barks and Meows app on your device
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" button
  4. Tap on "Delete Account"
  5. Read the confirmation message carefully
  6. Confirm deletion by tapping "Delete" in the dialog

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your email address and authentication credentials
  • Pet Profiles: All pet information including names, ages, and categories
  • Vaccine Records: All vaccination notes, dates, and doctor information
  • App Preferences: Any settings or preferences you've configured

Data Retention Period

Immediate Deletion: All your data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. There is no retention period.

However, please note:

  • Backup copies in our systems may take up to 30 days to be fully removed
  • Some data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention)

Before You Delete

Before deleting your account, please consider:

  • This action is permanent - You cannot recover your account or data after deletion
  • Export your data - Currently, we don't offer data export. Please save any important information manually before deletion
  • Alternative options - If you're having issues with the app, consider logging out instead, or contact us for support

Alternative: Logout

If you want to stop using the app temporarily but keep your data, you can simply logout instead:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap "Logout"

Your data will remain safe and you can log back in anytime.
