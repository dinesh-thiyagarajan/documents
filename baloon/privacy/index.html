Privacy Policy for Baloon

Last updated: 06/02/2026

Baloon ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Baloon mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Overview

Baloon is a casual hot air balloon game. The App is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not require user registration or account creation.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • High Score: Your best game score is saved on your device using Android DataStore to provide a persistent high score experience.

This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for use by children as it does not require any personal data.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of data breach from our end. All game data remains on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
