Privacy Policy

Peyar - Tamil Name Generator

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

Peyar ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Tamil name generator application handles information.

Key Point: Peyar is a completely offline, privacy-focused app that does NOT collect, transmit, or share any user data. All functionality operates locally on your device.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Peyar stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Favorite Names: When you mark names as favorites, they are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore
  • App Preferences: Basic app settings and preferences are stored locally
Important: All data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Peyar is designed with privacy in mind. We explicitly do NOT:

  • Collect any personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Collect usage analytics or statistics
  • Use third-party tracking services or analytics tools
  • Require account creation or login
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Upload any data to external servers

Internet Permissions

Peyar does NOT require or use internet permissions. The app functions entirely offline using:

  • AI-Generated Names: Generated locally on your device using an embedded TensorFlow Lite machine learning model
  • Traditional Names: Loaded from datasets bundled within the app
  • No Network Communication: The app never connects to the internet or any external servers

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure DataStore
  • Your favorite names and preferences are only accessible by the app
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • No data is backed up to cloud services unless you have enabled Android system backups

Children's Privacy

Peyar does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Peyar does not integrate with or use any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Machine Learning Model

The app includes an embedded TensorFlow Lite machine learning model that:

  • Runs entirely on your device
  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Generates Tamil names locally without requiring internet connectivity
  • Does not collect or store training data from your usage

Your Rights

Since Peyar does not collect any personal information or transmit data outside your device, your privacy is fully protected by default. You have complete control over:

  • Favorites: You can add or remove favorite names at any time
  • App Data: You can clear all app data through Android settings
  • Uninstallation: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

Data Retention

  • Favorite names are retained on your device until you remove them or uninstall the app
  • No data is retained by us as we do not collect or have access to any user data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Summary
