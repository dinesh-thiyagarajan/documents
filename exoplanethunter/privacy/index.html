Exoplanet Hunter does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App. We do not collect:

The App may store game data locally on your device. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

4. Data Storage and Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. This data:
