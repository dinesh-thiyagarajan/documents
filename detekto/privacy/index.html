Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

Welcome to Detekto ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Detekto (the "App").

Summary: Detekto is a utility app that displays your current signal strength and the signal strength of nearby providers. We do not collect personal information. The App uses third-party advertising services that may collect anonymous data for ad personalization.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Detekto does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App. We do not collect:

  • Names or usernames
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Payment information

1.2 Device and Network Information

The App reads signal strength and network provider information directly from your device in order to display it to you. This data is:

  • Processed locally on your device only
  • Not transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Not stored or logged by us

1.3 Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • General location (country/region level)

This information is collected and used by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

The signal strength and network data accessed by the App is used solely to:

  • Display your current signal strength
  • Show signal strength of available network providers
  • Provide a real-time signal monitoring experience

3. Advertising

Detekto displays advertisements to support the free availability of the App. We use Google AdMob as our advertising partner.

3.1 Ad Personalization

Google may use the collected data to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Adjusting your device's advertising settings
  • Visiting Google's Ads Settings
  • Using your device's "Opt out of Ads Personalization" feature

4. Data Storage and Security

Detekto does not store any personal data. Signal and network information is read in real time and displayed to you without being saved or transmitted. Any app preferences are stored locally on your device and:

  • Remain on your device only
  • Are not backed up to cloud services by default
  • Are deleted when you uninstall the App

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not include social features or chat
  • Does not allow in-app purchases

Parents can manage ad personalization settings through their child's device settings.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads through device settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the App and all associated data at any time

7. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain advertisements that link to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Posting the updated policy in the App or on our website

Your continued use of the App after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Permissions

Detekto requires the following permissions to function:

  • Phone State: Required to read signal strength and network provider information
  • Internet Access: Required for displaying advertisements
  • Network State: Used to check connectivity and display network details

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or other sensitive permissions beyond what is necessary for the App's core functionality.
